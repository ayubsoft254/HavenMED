Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about HavenMED services.

How do I book a home visit?

You can book a home visit through our mobile app, website, or by calling our support line. Simply select your preferred provider, choose a time slot, and confirm your booking.

Are all providers verified?

Yes, all healthcare providers on HavenMED undergo thorough verification including license checks, background verification, and continuous quality monitoring.

What are your consultation fees?

Virtual consultations start from KSh 300, while home visits range from KSh 800-1,500 depending on the provider and services required.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with major insurance providers in Kenya. Check with our support team to confirm if your insurance plan is accepted.

How can I become a provider?

Healthcare professionals can apply through our provider portal. You'll need to submit your credentials, undergo verification, and complete our onboarding process.

Is emergency care available?

Yes, we have 24/7 emergency support. For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 first, then contact our emergency line for additional support.
