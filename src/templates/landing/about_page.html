The Vision Behind HavenMED

Janet Owino founded HavenMED with a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure that quality healthcare is accessible to every Kenyan, regardless of their location or economic status.

Having witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by both patients struggling to access care and qualified healthcare professionals seeking meaningful employment opportunities, Janet recognized the need for a platform that could bridge this gap.

"Healthcare is a fundamental human right, not a privilege," says Janet. "Through HavenMED, we're not just connecting patients with providers—we're building a sustainable ecosystem that empowers communities and transforms lives."

"Every patient deserves access to quality healthcare, and every qualified healthcare professional deserves the opportunity to practice their calling. HavenMED makes both possible."
— Janet Owino, Founder & CEO

Our Mission & Vision

Driven by purpose, guided by values, and focused on transforming healthcare in Kenya.

Our Mission

To democratize healthcare access in Kenya by connecting patients with verified, underutilized healthcare professionals through innovative technology, making quality medical care affordable, accessible, and available to all.

  • Bridge healthcare accessibility gaps
  • Empower healthcare professionals
  • Ensure quality and affordability

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading healthcare platform, where every individual has seamless access to quality medical care, and every healthcare professional can thrive in their practice while serving their communities.

  • Regional healthcare leadership
  • Universal healthcare access
  • Sustainable healthcare ecosystem
